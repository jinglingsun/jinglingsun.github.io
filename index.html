About me

Jingling Sun is a Lecturer of School of Information and Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), China (2023-). Previously, she received Ph.D. and B.S. degrees from East China Normal University (ECNU), Shanghai, China (2014-2023), and supervised by Prof. Geguang Pu and Prof. Ting Su. She also had one-year visit in Singapore Management University (SMU), Singapore (2022-2023), and was advised by Prof. Jun Sun.
